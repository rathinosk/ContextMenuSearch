Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how our browser extension, ContextMenuSearch, handles user data.

Data Collection

Our extension does not collect any user data. All operations are performed locally on your device.

Data Usage

Since no data is collected, there is no data usage to disclose.

Data Sharing

Our extension does not share any user data with any third parties. All interactions are confined to the configurations set by the user.

Accessibility

This privacy policy is accessible via the link provided in the designated Chrome Web Store Developer Dashboard field.
